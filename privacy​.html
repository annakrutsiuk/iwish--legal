Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-29 · Last updated: 2026-04-29

iWish is developed by Anna Krutsiuk, an individual developer ("I", "me", or "my"). I respect your privacy and am committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the iWish mobile application (the "App").

This policy is designed to comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other applicable data protection laws.

1. Information I Collect

a) Information you provide directly:

  • Account information: name, email address, and password when you register
  • Profile information: username, profile photo
  • User content: wishlists, wish items, links, notes, and images you add to the App
  • Communications: messages you send to me via support email

b) Information collected automatically:

  • Device information: device model, operating system version
  • Crash reports and diagnostic data (via Firebase Crashlytics)

c) Information from third parties:

  • When you sign in using Apple or Google, I receive basic profile information (name, email) as authorized by those providers.

2. How I Use Your Information

I use your information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App
  • Create and manage your account
  • Authenticate users and secure the App
  • Save your wishlists and preferences
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Detect and fix technical issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

Legal bases for processing (GDPR): I process your data based on your consent, the performance of our contract (providing the App), my legitimate interests, and legal obligations.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect or process your data:

  • Apple (Sign in with Apple): authentication. See Apple's Privacy Policy
  • Google (Google Sign-In / Firebase): authentication, database, and crash reporting. See Google's Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Firestore: stores your wishlists and profile data securely in the cloud

4. Data Sharing

I do not sell your personal data. I do not share your information with third parties except:

  • With the third-party services listed above, which are necessary to operate the App
  • To comply with legal obligations or governmental requests
  • To protect the rights or safety of users or others

When you share a wishlist profile link with friends, those friends can view your public wishlist data.

5. Data Retention

I retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to provide the App's services. When you delete your account, your personal data will be removed from active systems within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Right of access: request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to rectification: request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure: request deletion of your data
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

California residents (CCPA): you have the right to know what data is collected, to delete it, and to opt out of the sale of personal information (I do not sell personal information).

To exercise any of these rights, contact me at support.iwish@gmail.com.

7. Account & Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the App: Profile → Settings → Delete Account.

You may also request deletion by emailing support.iwish@gmail.com. Once deleted, your personal data will be removed from active systems within 30 days.

8. Data Security

I implement appropriate technical measures to protect your personal data, including encrypted transmission (HTTPS) and Firebase security rules. However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure, and I cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If I learn that such data has been collected, I will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When I do, I will revise the "Last Updated" date above. Your continued use of the App after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact me at:
support.iwish@gmail.com
